Bungo Stray Dogs
Having been kicked out of the orphanage and on the verge of starvation,
Atsushi Nakajima meets some men. They are both members of the Armed Detective

mission. In the city of Yokohama there are people who bear the names of great

This is the start of the battle between the mysterious powers of the Armed
Detective Agency and the Port Mafia..

Chillin' in My 30s after Getting Fired from the Demon King's Army
Dariel, a soldier in the Demon King's army who cannot use magic,
has managed to rise to become an assistant to the Four Generals thanks to
his talent and energy, where he always put his skills to good use. However,
when they replace the Four Generals, Dariel is fired from the army. Dariel is
devastated, but upon reaching a human village, he discovers that his adventuring
skills, useless to demons, are just what he needs! "Was I... human?" Dariel decides
to start a new life in this town and requests do not stop coming!.
